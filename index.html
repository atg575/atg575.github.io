Rivian Projects

Battery Interconnect Fatigue Testing

Spearheaded the fatigue testing regiment used to validate battery module current collector to cell interconnect weld reliability.

  • Designed coupons and test setup, ran both quasi-static and fatigue tests, and compared empirical results against CAE modeled requirements to ensure long-term durability.
  • Collaborated with product engineers to integrate test findings into design improvements.
  • Broke down the improvements and significant findings to leadership and documented the results and methodology for future reference.

Weld Clamp Design

Designed and commissioned a set of retrofit weld clamps to replicate production machine clamping footprints on a patent-pending weld mask used on the module pilot line.

  • Used a drafted interior oriface to assist the vision targeting system in placing precise welds.
  • Collaborated with product engineers to integrate test findings into design improvements.
  • Broke down the improvements and significant findings to leadership and documented the results and methodology for future reference.

Module Busbar Heat Stake Rework Testing

Conducted comparison trials for the rework adhesive used to adhere busbars to the module frame, testing various application methods and volume distributions.

  • Determined most efficient volume and distribution of adhesive to bond busbars to frames
  • Strength tested adhesive rework setups and compared to heat stakes of varying heights and staking pressures.
  • Created a Job Execution Sheet instructing technicians on how to complete the rework process.
